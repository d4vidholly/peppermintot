About


Becky is dedicated to unlocking every child's potential through personalised, play-based therapy. She is passionate about fostering growth, building skills, and creating joyful learning experiences.

  • Specialist in children and young people: Expertise in neurodevelopmental disorders, intellectual and psychosocial disabilities.
  • 5+ years of clinical experience: delivering trauma-informed and strength-based intervention across community, hospitals and secure care settings.
  • NDIS and assessment knowledge: In-depth understanding of NDIS pathways and skilled in functional capacity assessments.

Becky holds AHPRA Registration and is committed to ongoing professional development to remain up to date with the most effective evidence based practice and creative solutions.

Services


PeppermintOT offers a range of services designed to meet the needs of children and young people. The following Assessments are available for Plan Managed and Self Managed NDIS participants only. NDIS OT services are $193.99 per hour (or per price guide). Travel will be charged as per NDIS price guide.

  • Functional Capacity Assessments (FCA) evaluate abilities in daily life, providing a detailed report to outline necessary support.
  • Home and Living Assessments (SDA/SIL/ILO) evaluate housing needs, daily tasks, and support, to identify modifications for a safe, accessible environment.

We also offer Occupational Therapy Practice Supervision for New Graduates and/or Overseas Qualified OTs seeking full AHPRA registration. Please get in touch for more information.
